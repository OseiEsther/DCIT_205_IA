DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

The University of Ghana's Computer Science Department fosters innovation and excellence through dynamic learning where faculty industry experts lead students in hands-on programming, AI, cybersecurity, and more, preparing future tech leaders to shape the future via research and solving real-world challenges.

Preparing Our Students to Make Meaningful Contributions to the World

The main goal of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana is to provide a comprehensive and rigorous education in computer science that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values needed to excel in the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Undergraduate students have the option of declaring a Bachelor of Science or a Minor in Computer Science. Graduate students have the opportunity to pursue a Master's or PhD degree in Computer Science. The Master's degree is a terminal professional degree. The PhD is for those who desire a research or teaching career.
